About Us

About TMH

TMH is an NGO dedicated to making mental healthcare more accessible in India, aiming to break the stigma surrounding mental health. The organization provides education, awareness, workshops, events, and counseling, creating a supportive environment for people to seek help and express themselves freely. TMH operates online to reach a global audience and plans to expand into offline initiatives, aiming to provide inclusive mental health care and foster a healthier generation.

About the Core Team

TMH was founded by Bhumi Aggarwal and Vaani Gupta, with Satvik Singhal as the Director. Inspired by the need for a safe space, the team focuses on mental health awareness and support, primarily targeting teenagers. Bhumi, a NEET aspirant and social worker, brings passion and knowledge to the organization. Vaani, a creative commerce student, leads with enthusiasm, while Satvik, an experienced MUN participant, contributes his expertise in social initiatives.

Future Plans

TMH is focused on addressing mental health issues across India. They have hosted competitions, collaborated with events like MUNs, and plan to partner with schools to create supportive environments for students. Future initiatives include summer courses, community events for fundraising, free therapy sessions, podcasts, peer support groups, and art and music therapy.

SDG Goals

TMH aligns with several Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including Good Health and Well-Being (SDG 3), Quality Education (SDG 4), Reduced Inequalities (SDG 10), and Partnerships for the Goals (SDG 17). Their vision is to create a world where mental health is recognized as a vital component of overall wellness.
